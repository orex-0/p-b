Team B

Team B other wise known as the medical team, is responsible for providing immediate medical assistance during emergencies in an attempt to safely get us to the hospital. Usually they are forgotten after the initial response, but their contributions are equally vital to saving lives

The forgotten heroes of emergencies, the medical team is often overshadowed by the more visible efforts of the police and firefighters. However, their role in providing critical medical care and ensuring the well-being of those affected cannot be overstated. They are the ones who work tirelessly behind the scenes, administering first aid, stabilizing patients, and coordinating with hospitals to ensure a smooth transition of care. Their dedication and expertise are essential in saving lives and minimizing the impact of emergencies on individuals and communities. We should recognize and appreciate the invaluable contributions of the medical team in our emergency response efforts.

They play a crucial role in protecting lives and property during emergencies. Their bravery and dedication are commendable, as they often put themselves in harm's way to save others. We should recognize and appreciate the important work of firefighters in our communities.

Team D

Team D also known as the search and rescue teams, are responsible for locating and rescuing individuals who are lost, trapped, or in danger during emergencies. They play a critical role in saving lives and providing assistance to those in need. Team E

Team E also known as the disaster response teams, are responsible for providing assistance and support during natural disasters and other large-scale emergencies. We should recognize and appreciate the important work of emergency management teams in our communities.
